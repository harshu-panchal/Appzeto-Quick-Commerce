## ðŸ“ Writing Rules

### Length
- **Sentences:** Max 20 words
- **Buttons:** 1-3 words
- **Labels:** 1-4 words
- **Descriptions:** 1-2 sentences

### Voice
- **Use active voice:** "We'll send your order"
- **Not passive:** "Your order will be sent"

### Tone
- **Be friendly:** "Welcome back!"
- **Not formal:** "Authentication successful"

### Clarity
- **Be specific:** "Enter your email"
- **Not vague:** "Provide credentials"
